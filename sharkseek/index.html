SHARK SEEK

OVERVIEW

Tired of the same ol boring pets? Need a bit more teeth with your cuddles? Then check out SHARK SEEK! The new companion app for people looking for a more loveable and vicious pet. Shark Seek pairs you with available sharks located mere miles away. Great Whites, Hammerheads, Whale Sharks, WE HAVE IT ALL. Find your shark today!

TYPOGRAPHY

For headlines, I used Brandon grotesque for that warm and welcoming feel. For the smaller headlines and body copy, I used a Titling Gothic FB, a beautiful grotesque san serif… You know, so I could fit all the words on the page.

COLORS

Blues for days! Dark blues! Light blues! Greeny Blues! Because… Well, it’s an ocean themed app. Duh!
