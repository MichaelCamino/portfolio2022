OVERVIEW

I graduated from the New Media Arts program at Kapiolani Community College. The program specialized in Interface Design. Graphic design, web design, UI/UX. We even dip our little toes into animation, theory and film. What I'm trying to say is, the other students in the cohort and I did a lot of work those 2 years and I wanted to make a video that showcased the students work. My work. Everyone's work. I also wanted to make something that Kapiolani Community College could possibly use as advertisement in the future.
