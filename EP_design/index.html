OVERVIEW

Ok, this was a fun project. Not only did I get to do a complete identity for a brand new bar in Honolulu, but I got to build them a website from scratch as well. They already had the logo, so all I had to do was fill in the blanks. Everything from copy-writing to packaging.

TYPOGRAPHY

For type and such, I really wanted the vibe of a magazine spread. Something you would find in a fashion mag. I ended up using a messy casual script font for the headlines. To contrast the headlines, I used a sophisticated geometric san serif for the body copy. I even threw a third condensed typeface into the website to spice things up. Can you find it?

COLORS

Gold was the color of day. I was going for “sophisticated low brow” so I chose more of a yellowish gold. I supplemented it with an off white and an off black and I think the color palette turned out pretty well.
